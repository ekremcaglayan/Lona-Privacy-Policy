Privacy Policy

At Lona Grill House Limited, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us when using our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you place an order, we may collect your name, contact information (email and phone number), and delivery details.
  • Payment Information: We do not store your credit card details. All payment transactions are securely handled by our payment provider.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use our app, such as order history and app interactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To communicate with you about your order, promotions, or updates (with your consent).
  • To improve our services and app functionality.
  • To comply with legal obligations if required.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.
  • Your information may be shared with trusted service providers who help us operate our app and process payments, under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

  • We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

5. Your Rights

  • You may access, update, or request deletion of your personal information by contacting us at the details below.
  • You can opt out of receiving promotional communications at any time.
